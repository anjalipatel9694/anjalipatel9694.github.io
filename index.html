PowerBI Dashboard: Eccommerce Sales Data Analysis

I led an in-depth sales data analysis project for Madhav Store, focusing on understanding product performance, profitability, and customer behavior. Leveraging detailed transaction records encompassing Order ID, Amount, Profit, Quantity, Category, and Payment Mode alongside additional customer information like Order Date, Customer Name, State, and City, our analysis encompassed various dimensions. By scrutinizing sales trends, we uncovered intriguing insights, discovering both high-profit and low-profit products across diverse categories. We delved into customer purchasing patterns, notably examining preferred payment modes and regional sales disparities. Our findings highlighted opportunities to optimize pricing strategies for underperforming items, proposed targeted marketing tactics for high-performing categories, and suggested exploring incentives for specific payment modes to enhance overall profitability. Ultimately, this analysis offered actionable recommendations to refine product offerings and sales strategies for Madhav Store.

Weather Analysis and Prediction on Weather dataset

In this project, I conducted a comprehensive analysis of historical weather data using Python's pandas and numpy libraries to explore, clean, and preprocess the dataset. Leveraging powerful visualization tools like matplotlib and seaborn, I created visual representations to understand the correlations and trends between weather parameters such as temperature, humidity, rainfall, and wind. Utilizing statistical and machine learning techniques from scikit-learn, I performed predictive modeling on the weather dataset. After splitting the data into training and testing sets using scikit-learn's train-test split, I employed regression or other appropriate models to predict weather conditions based on various parameters. Throughout the project, I used pandas and numpy for data manipulation, matplotlib and seaborn for data visualization, and scikit-learn for machine learning tasks such as model training, testing, and prediction.
